Miyoko was so happy that she knew Ms. Ha’s son could not repay a loan shark, so she promised to help him deal with his debts and used her financial strength to gain a voice in both families.

Those years in the yakuza taught Miyoko the truth: first let others owe her, she can pry a bigger cake.

So Miyoko, who had settled the loan sharks, began to extort money from the two families frequently, and according to the investigation, in just two years, Miyoko pocketed all the property.

This is the end of the story, right?

But then, Ms. Xia’s third son couldn’t afford to pay back the money Miyoko lent him to repay the loan sharks and ran away.

Miyoko was still worried about how to find the next breakthrough, and with this incident, she morally suppressed the two families.

She began to try to apply the same kind of regulatory tactics from the yakuza to the family.

The yakuza are good at interrogation, destroying a person’s willpower with various forms of violence, and Miyoko has learned these techniques to a tee.

While violently guarding and interrogating the two families, she picked two of the younger ones to adopt as her sons.

The neighbors reported the case several times, but the police rejected it on the grounds that “internal family conflicts are not dealt with”.

The two poor families were torn apart by the endless abuse.

In 1999, Ms. Xia was tortured to death by her three sons, and Miyoko was already very skilled in the art of murder by knife.

Takeshi Kadokoro gathered the courage to turn himself in at the police station, but the witnesses were too afraid of Miyoko to come forward, and the family was too afraid to confess because of their involvement in Ms. Natsu’s murder, and the last report, which ended in failure, was made.

Once again, Miyoko easily escaped the law. Having killed two people in a row without being convicted, Miyoko became more and more satisfied with her abilities.

Her inflated self-confidence made the crime more and more rampant.

“Which family should I start with next? Let’s do something more exciting!”

05

“An exciting blood slaughter”

After destroying three families in a row, Miyoko slowly found the knack to evolve into a skilled murderer.

She knows very well that the families she can directly or indirectly hook up with are the best dishes to be harvested.

Taking advantage of the weaknesses of human society and following the trail, she could always find the next family.

So she targeted her ex-husband’s circle of friends, using the natural goodwill of relatives to get close to each other.

Miyoko went from picking the family to penetrating the inner circle with increasing speed and a steady and precise entry point.

The Kaikichi family would never have imagined that an ordinary exchange of pleasantries would become an invitation to death.

The Kaikichi’s eldest son and Miyoko’s ex-husband were alumni from high school, and Miyoko quickly climbed into conversation with the other.

In 2001, the two met and kept in touch.

In 2002, Miyoko began to visit the Kaikichi’s home several times.

Remember at the beginning when we introduced Miyoko’s experience of opening a porn bar?

Miyoko used her old ability to seduce men in this hunt. She was so skilled in her business that she charmed the Kaikichi’s eldest son and invited the Kaikichi’s eldest and second sons to come and live in her apartment together.

It is worth mentioning that this apartment of hers was originally the property of the Hashimoto family.

After 11 months of living together, Miyoshi’s eldest son was compelled by Miyoko to quit his job and Miyoko began to reveal her true nature.

The two brothers who came to their senses succeeded in escaping in 2007 and changed their names to begin a long life on the run.

With Miyoko’s character, the rest of the Kaikichi family could not be spared.

In 2003, the 87-year-old mother of the Kaikichi family, who stayed at home, was taken by Miyoko to Takamatsu City, Kagawa, where she was imprisoned and abused to death, and her remains were buried near the Tanimoto family home in Takamatsu City.

The strange thing is.

Miyoko never did anything meaningless, so why did she have to take an 87-year-old man all the way to Takamatsu City, Kagawa, which is very far away?

After combing through a large number of cases, I found that Miyoko was becoming more and more skillful in manipulating her family, not only destroying families at a faster pace, but also maturing in her methods and abilities.

She even started a two-pronged plan to destroy the next family in the process of “finishing” the last one.

That’s right, Miyoko is imprisoning the Kaikichi family while looking for her next family.

She is no longer satisfied with eating the grass on the edge, she wants to verify something bigger.

The unfortunate Tanimoto family is the first “stranger” family Miyoko tries.

The Tanimoto family was Miyoko’s penultimate crime before she was caught, marking the full maturity of Miyoko’s methods.

It was scalable, batchable, and so efficient that the process was already fixed.

The Tanimoto family and the Oe family mentioned at the beginning are very ordinary, ordinary people, and Miyoko can be considered strangers.

How to bring down a strange family? After thinking about it day and night, Miyoko cut into the family as a friend.

The kind and happy Tanimoto family of four could not have imagined that the family would be completely destroyed by this old woman whom they knew from one side.

The police information on the Tanimoto family murder is not comprehensive, after reviewing a lot of information, I will restore the most critical part of the case.

Mr. Tanimoto and his wife, Mrs. Hatsushiro, had two daughters, Jasmine, the eldest, and Rukia, the youngest.

No one could have imagined that years later, the younger daughter Rukia would become the murderer of her sister Jasmineko.

Not only did Rukia recognize Miyoko as her mother and obey her, but she also married Miyoko’s adopted son and became a complete accomplice.

(The four members of the Tanimoto family, left sister Rukia and right sister Jasako) In 2003, Miyoko frequently visited the Tanimoto family in Takamatsu City as a friend. In the morning, she was laughing and joking with the Tanimoto family, but in the afternoon, she went to the house in Takamatsu City to abuse the elderly mother of the Kaikichi family.

With the fat of the Tanimoto family, Miyoko was not slow to give the Kaikichi family to wrap up the disposal of their property and destroy the body.

From controlling the family to completely appropriating the Tanimoto family’s property, this time, Miyoko took less than half a year.

She brought 10 socialites to the Tanimoto family and stayed there for half a year, violently driving the Tanimoto family to live on the second floor to prevent them from escaping and putting the yakuza on duty in shifts every day.

Locking the defenseless women in the hut, they inflicted violence while mentally pressuring them with.

“You should know exactly what you are, you are a disgrace to society!”

She made the family members write down bad things about each other, mastered the conflicts between each of them, called the younger ones out to talk alone, instilled bad things about the elders, and forced them to be loyal to herself.

The family then became a scene of holding each other back and targeting each other, and Miyoko established a new order without having to watch over it herself.

Every morning the Tanimoto family was forced to produce 200,000 yen in cash, and in just six months Miyoko took more than 20 million yen (equivalent to about 1.2 million yuan).

She made the family kill each other, taking the younger generation as hostages to threaten the elders to obey, and everyone was unable to resist.

In contrast, her sister Jasmineko was personally strong-willed and was not brainwashed by Miyoko.

Mr. Tanimoto realizes that there is only one chance to ask for help with this tight control of Miyoko! He found the right moment to pretend to quarrel with his wife, Ms. Hatsushiro, and took advantage of the chaos to let Jasako escape. The confused Jasako was ready to go to take the train to leave the city, but the train required identity registration and was arrested back by Miyoko on the grounds that her family was running amok.

Her sister, Jasmine, is not at all obedient, so Miyoko has her sister, Rukia, who has been completely brainwashed, take her to her apartment in Amagasaki City and imprison her, where Rukia recognizes Miyoko as her mother and marries Miyoko’s son, becoming a member of the Kakuta family.

Rukia abused Jasako more and more, burning herself with a firearm and burning herself with a cigarette was a common occurrence.

The neighbors had called the police as many as 36 times, and like the victims of other cases, the police refused to investigate on the grounds that the case could not be filed if not reported by the victim herself.

Like Kaiai Oe, Jasako was surprisingly lucky to escape, and she moved to Osaka to live her life anonymously and anew, unwilling to call the police because of her own involvement in the abuse of her family.

Jasako’s quiet life lasted for 2 years and 11 months.

December 2006.

Also for the reason of changing her driver’s license, the police informed Miyoko of Jasako’s whereabouts. Jasako was once again taken back by her sister who had come a long way and was brutally abused to death, and her mother, Ms. Hatsushiro, was also abused to death by her sister in her old home.

After appropriating all of the Tanimoto family’s property and stealing a daughter as a family member and killing Tanimoto’s wife and eldest daughter, Miyoko was completely satisfied to call it quits.

During the same year, Miyoko set her sights on the unrelated Oe family …………

After sorting through all the cases, I realized that Miyoko did not start off with such a perfect crime.

She had periods of trial and error, but because she firmly grasped the psychology of the murdered families, exploited the loopholes in the police and the law, got away with it again and again, and refined her criminal techniques to perfection, she was able to create seven tragic cases that spanned 25 years.

Behind the seven cases, Miyoko also has something different from ordinary serial killers.

She clearly came for her fortune, but then she forced the families, who were drained of their economic value, to die one by one.

It seems to be some kind of pleasure for her to break the superficial harmony between human beings, and to see the family members being suspicious of each other may be one of her motives for committing the crime.

What kind of mood did Miyoko have to commit her crime?

What was her real motive? With Miyoko’s suicide after her arrest, there is no way to know.

06

" The Final Trial and the End "

On September 25, 2013, the last case opened in Kobe.

The 25-year-long “Idea Serial Murder Case”, in which at least seven families were completely destroyed, finally came to an end.

All the members of the family who were directly or indirectly involved in the killings were convicted to varying degrees.

The Kagawa Prefectural Police Department, which ignored 36 calls from the murdered families, publicly admitted its inadequacy, and as the case shocked Japanese society, the Hyogo Prefectural Police and the Kagawa Prefectural Police set up a new department to analyze the inquiries received by each police station.

Japan has also introduced policies related to disputes between relatives and neighbors.

The media have speculated about Miyoko Kakuta’s motive for the crime, but it remains foggy.

Regarding Miyoko as a person, there are some contradictions in her unusual modus operandi and motive that feel reasonable but perverse that deserve attention.

Combined with criminal psychology and police speculation, most murderers with strange personalities are used to living alone.

Instead, Miyoko formed an extended family with eight unrelated people throughout the series of cases. From the narrative of the previous cases, we can see that she has an obsession with children and likes to adopt them. And this group of her family members rely on her to make ends meet.

On one hand, she is constantly destroying other people’s families, and on the other hand, she sets out to build her own family.

Miyoko seems to keep proving the dark side of human nature and the fragility of love in various cruel ways in different families. This also reinforces her belief in the pessimistic ideas she previously subscribed to.

Every time she provoked family relationships, she watched silently. Sometimes with silent tears on the sidelines, other times with a self-deprecating smile.

For 25 years, the collapse of every family seemed to start with the provocation of the couple’s relationship, and every couple’s family was her testing ground. And each couple’s relationship eventually, not surprisingly, broke down, with both sides even fist-fighting.

As events progress to a later stage, this break-up gradually rises again between the two generations, intensifying. Throughout the spectatorship, Miyoko seems to be watching everything from God’s perspective.

Combined with Miyoko’s family of origin and early childhood experiences, one cannot help but venture a guess that Miyoko may have been subconsciously reverting to the traumatic experiences of her childhood, to the episodes where her family had a significant impact on her psyche.

Perhaps she is verifying, trying to repeatedly confirm whether there is a true love that will not break, whether there is a love in the family that can never be provoked?

Or maybe she is just making herself admit over and over again in a case that really happened, admitting that the pessimism in her own eyes is what the world was supposed to be like ……

Perhaps the world in Miyoko’s eyes can be touched with one sentence.

When you get used to pessimism, it can be as pleasant as optimism.
