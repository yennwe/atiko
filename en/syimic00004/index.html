Chapter 4: His Kindness, Given to Only One

Lu Siyi left first.

Gu Shisheng went to a nearby caf√© alone for a while, then took the circular bus. Somehow, he ended up at the entrance of their high school.

Gu Shisheng got off the bus there.

Since it was during the holidays, the school entrance was deserted. Unable to enter, he stood outside, took a few glances, circled around, and found a small eatery to sit down, ordering a bowl of rice noodles.

The rice noodles were very spicy, and Gu Shisheng didn’t know when his eyes had turned red from the spiciness.

His phone rang, a strange number sending a photo.

The picture was clear: his husband, in a dim underground parking lot, lowered his head to kiss a handsome young man. The young man’s eyes were full of charm, while Lu Siyi looked emotionally involved, his expression dark, clearly showing signs of affection.

Gu Shisheng remained quite calm, even enlarging the photo to get a closer look at the young man. He was very young, seemingly in his early twenties‚Äîyouthful and exciting. Freshness and stimulation would undoubtedly be more abundant.

His husband was still wearing the same clothes as when he left.

The tie that the young man held in his hand was the one Gu Shisheng bought for Lu Siyi as a birthday gift last year. It was precious to the point that Lu Siyi only wore it for their dates.

Setting down the phone, Gu Shisheng inadvertently took a bite of something spicy, and in an instant, tears welled up from the heat.

The young man skillfully helped Lu Siyi put on his clothes, sat up, wiped his mouth with a tissue, and smiled, “President Lu, they said because of their mistake, they’ll give us another five percent.”

Lu Siyi lit a cigarette and, hearing this, glanced at the young man, “Muyan, there’s a project on the zr City side. Go handle it first.”

Muyan’s original smile vanished in an instant, looking at Lu Siyi with grievances, “Senior, I just came back.”

Muyan graduated from the same school as Lu Siyi and Gu Shisheng, but several years later. In his first year at the university, he heard about Lu Siyi’s name and even saw his photo, hanging as the first outstanding graduate in their school, with Gu Shisheng as the second.

The two had a magnificent love story, including how a proud son of heaven like Lu Siyi could love an ordinary person like Gu Shisheng.

Muyan admired them deeply and worked hard. At that time, he didn’t think of destroying their marriage.

But one year later, when Lu Siyi came to the school for a speech, he realized that someone could be even more stunning than in photos. Muyan was love-struck, working hard until he entered the Lu family’s group after graduating.

He worked hard step by step to be by Lu Siyi’s side, not just to become a disposable pet.

After complaining, Muyan saw Lu Siyi’s cold gaze, and his body trembled slightly as he nodded.

He understood Lu Siyi too well. This person wasn’t as kind as he appeared; the true nature was inherently wicked. Those pets who threw tantrums when pampered a little were the most miserable ones in the end, and Muyan knew that.

Lu Siyi’s gentle kindness was given to only one person.

Muyan felt resentment in his heart but dared not say anything on the surface.

If someone was naturally indifferent and didn’t love anyone, he would accept it. However, how could one find balance in their heart after experiencing the difference in treatment from this person? Such a person, loving someone, turned out to be so good.

Those who had been Lu Siyi’s lovers all peeked at the position of Mrs. Lu.

But those who exposed their thoughts were all dealt with by Mr. Lu.

This man would never allow his wife to discover someone outside.

But…

Did he really not know anything?

Lu Siyi thought his wife was fragile, but it seemed like he could withstand a blow after all.
